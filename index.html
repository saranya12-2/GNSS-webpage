About IITTNiF

Positioning and Precision Technologies (PPTs) play a crucial role in integrating and analyzing spatial and temporal data...

The Hub will address all phases of the technology lifecycle — Knowledge, Development, Translation, and Commercialization — focusing on self-reliance in PPT.

The TIH will primarily adopt a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model to generate revenue through:

  • R&D sponsorship from industry, government, and start-ups.
  • Linkages with industries, accelerators, and venture capital for funding ecosystems.
  • Training and consulting services.
  • Development of standards and policies for rapid adoption of PPT.
  • Creation of databanks in strategic PPT areas.

The Hub aims to develop innovative solutions for grand challenges in Positioning and Precision Technologies...
